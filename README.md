I'm currently in the early stage of my engineering career — pursuing a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning while working as a Frontend Engineering Intern at **Prodesk IT**. My approach to development is grounded in clarity: I care less about reaching for the newest tool and more about whether an interface actually feels right to use, holds up across screen sizes, and is easy for the next person to read and extend.

I'm drawn to products that look simple on the surface and are carefully considered underneath — clean layouts, deliberate spacing, and interactions that don't call attention to themselves. I try to bring that same discipline to every page I build, from structure down to the smallest detail.

Right now, I'm focused on strengthening my frontend fundamentals while building a real foundation in AI & Machine Learning, with the long-term goal of becoming a **Full Stack AI Engineer** — someone who can take a product from interface to intelligence, and contribute meaningfully to open-source along the way.
